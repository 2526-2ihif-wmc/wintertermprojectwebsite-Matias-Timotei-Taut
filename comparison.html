PCs vs Laptops

Choosing between a desktop computer and a laptop depends on your specific needs. Here's a detailed comparison to help you decide.

Desktop Computers

Power

More powerful processors and graphics cards available

Portability

Not portable, requires permanent setup

Price

Better value for performance

Upgrade Options

Highly upgradeable - easy to replace parts

Battery Life

Requires constant power connection

Laptops

Power

Less powerful due to size and heat constraints

Portability

Highly portable, work from anywhere

Price

More expensive for equivalent performance

Upgrade Options

Limited upgrades, mostly RAM and storage only

Battery Life

4-12 hours of portable use without power

Which Should You Choose?

Choose a Desktop if: You need maximum performance, want to upgrade components, or work primarily from one location.

Choose a Laptop if: You need portability, travel frequently, or have limited space for a full desktop setup.
